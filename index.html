Smart Radar System

Voice-Controlled Bot

Problem: Teleoperating robots in hazardous environments usually relies on complex manual controllers, which can distract the operator and limit hands-free usability.

Solution: Developed a wireless robotic vehicle controlled entirely via voice commands through a custom Android app over Bluetooth. Built on an ESP32 with an L298N motor driver, enabling intuitive, precise directional control. Prototyped and validated virtually on Tinkercad before physical assembly.

ESP32 Bluetooth L298N Motor Driver Arduino IDE Tinkercad

Posture Guard AI Web

Problem: Long work or study sessions often lead to unhealthy habits like slouching or forward neck bending. Traditional posture correction requires cumbersome wearable devices, making it hard to build healthy habits.

Solution: Developed a smart, browser-based application that leverages a standard webcam and computer vision to analyze body/face landmarks in real time. It estimates posture alignment and delivers immediate user-friendly alerts (visual/audio) when poor posture is detected—all without requiring any extra hardware.
